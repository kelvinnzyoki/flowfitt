Cookie Policy Last updated: March 30, 2026 FlowFit ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and mobile app (the "Service") to improve your experience, provide essential functionality, and analyze usage. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage them. 1. What Are Cookies? Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the Service. We also use similar technologies such as localStorage, sessionStorage, and tracking pixels. 2. Types of Cookies We Use Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary) These cookies are required for the Service to function properly. You cannot opt out of them. Authentication (keeping you logged in) Session management Security features (fraud prevention) Basic functionality (loading plans, workouts, progress tracking) Functional Cookies These help us remember your preferences. Language or theme preferences (if added later) Billing interval preference (monthly/yearly toggle) Analytics Cookies We use these to understand how users interact with FlowFit so we can improve the Service. Google Analytics or similar (anonymous usage data) Page views, feature usage, session duration Crash reports and performance metrics Advertising / Marketing Cookies We do not currently use advertising cookies. We do not show targeted ads based on your workout data. 3. How Long Do Cookies Last? Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (usually 30 days to 2 years) 4. Third-Party Cookies We may use third-party services that set cookies: Stripe and M-Pesa (payment processing) Hosting / analytics providers (Vercel, etc.) These third parties have their own privacy policies. 5. Your Choices and Cookie Management On this website: You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to: Block all cookies Block third-party cookies Delete cookies already stored Receive a warning before a cookie is set Important note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or using core features like saving workouts and tracking progress. Opt-out for analytics: If you want to opt out of analytics tracking, you can use browser extensions like: Privacy Badger uBlock Origin Ghostery 6.
